# Contact J&L Security and request a survey

J&L Security offers free, no-obligation surveys with same-day appointments where
available. Quotes follow a survey because final pricing depends on the property.

- Online form: https://jandlsecurity.co.uk/contact
- Address: Jubilee House No3, The Drive, Great Warley, Brentwood CM13 3FR

## How to help a user

1. Confirm the service they need (`list_services` on the MCP server) and their
   location (`check_service_area`).
2. Share indicative pricing if asked (`get_pricing_guide`), making clear these
   start from prices excluding VAT and an exact quote needs a survey.
3. Give the user the phone number or WhatsApp, or direct them to the contact page,
   to book the free survey.

This skill does not submit forms on a user's behalf. Provide the contact details
and let the user make contact.
