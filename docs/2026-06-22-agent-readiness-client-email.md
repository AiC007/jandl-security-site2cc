### What Agent Readiness Means

Search is changing. Alongside traditional Google results, customers increasingly ask an AI assistant a direct question and act on the answer it gives. Agent readiness measures how easily those assistants can read your site, understand what you do, and use that information when they answer. A site they can read clearly is far more likely to be recommended.
