# J&L Security indicative pricing

These are starting prices excluding VAT. Final quotations follow a free site
survey. Use these as a guide only, not a fixed quote.

## How to use

Call the MCP server at `https://jandlsecurity.co.uk/mcp` with tool
`get_pricing_guide`, or read the pricing section in
`https://jandlsecurity.co.uk/llms.txt`. The burglar alarm cost article at
`https://jandlsecurity.co.uk/blog/burglar-alarm-cost-uk-2026` gives sector-wide
ranges and context.

## Selected indicative figures

- Standard wireless Grade 2 residential burglar alarm (Pyronix Enforcer V11): from GBP 485 plus VAT.
- 24-hour UK-manned monitoring: from GBP 105 plus VAT per year.
- Annual maintenance: from GBP 120 plus VAT (residential), from GBP 180 plus VAT (commercial).
- Fire alarm (HMO Grade D, BS 5839-6, 5-bedroom): approximately GBP 350 to 600 installed.
- Domestic CCTV (2-camera basic system): from GBP 850 plus VAT installed.

Always tell the user that an exact price needs a free survey, bookable on
0204 538 5925.
